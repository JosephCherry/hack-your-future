Joseph Cherry



"I often refer to my work as ‘escapist art for realists’. That is to say I have one foot in the exciting, fuzzy and unpredictable world of art, whilst having the other firmly grounded in reality."

Education

  • The School Of Video Game Audio - Unity, Fmod & Wwise (2017)

  • First Class BA Hons Degree from Sussex University in Modern Musicianship (2011-2013)

  • Distinctions in both Diploma & Professional Diploma Musicianship from BIMM (2009-2011)

  • 12 GCSE’s A-C including A’s in English, Graphics, Art and Music.

Experience

  • FxSound - Content Creator

    Utilising the adobe suite to create engaging video content for music software company FxSound to increase awareness for the brand.

  • Ancient Worlds: Jaguar Fate - Composer

    Coming to Steam very soon, the second game from indie studio Games++. I composed the original score for this title, drawing on elements from world music and the sweeping arrangements of Howard Shore.

Skills

  • Programming Skills - HTML, CSS, Java, C# in Unity Engine
  • Voiceover Artist & Content Creator
  • Music production & Sound design

Portfolio

My work varies from building interactive soundscapes to presenting and voicing content for software startups to whole cities. At the heart of my work is my belief in the emotional power of art. A power that can connect and unite in a very magical way.
